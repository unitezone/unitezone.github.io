Xiaomi Mi Band Review

Leave a reply

Although not a household name outside of Asia. The Xiaomi made Mi band is the second most popular wearable globally.

Battery life
In daily usage I was able to get far more than the 30 days advertised. Having no screen & using Bluetooth 4

Accuracy
This is one area I think the Mi band can use some work. Each morning I normally start the day with over 100+ steps already logged & I don’t think its from sleep walking.

Features
The Mi band has a few key features that sets apart from a Fitbit. The Mi band can be used to automatically lock your phone if its out of range. Its best feature is a vibe setting when you get a call. This is great because if you have your phone on silent the band will still alert you to the incoming call.

Overall
The Xiaomi Mi band is a great fitness tracker for those just looking for a little push to exercise more. For the price under $30, features like call notifications, & battery life of over a month the Mi band is worth a try.
